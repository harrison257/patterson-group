About Kevin Patterson

A Legacy of Leadership
in Colorado Real Estate

CRS GRI SFR Broker / Owner
⚠ Review needed: Bio copy and stats below need approval before launch.

Kevin has an extensive background in sales and marketing, spending 16 years in management roles where he grew his real estate brokerage from 20 agents to 165. His company ranked among the top 250 brokerages in North America and the top 50 in the Prudential system. As the first Prudential affiliate in the Rocky Mountains, Kevin played a significant role in Prudential's expansion across Colorado.

After selling his interest in Prudential Professional REALTORS®, Kevin re-launched The Patterson Group as an independent boutique brokerage, building a new office at 3470 Centennial Boulevard in Colorado Springs. He has earned the Prudential Chairman's Circle Diamond Award, placing him in the top 1% of 75,000 brokers in North America.

A former U.S. Marine Corps Infantry Captain who also served as a White House Aide, Kevin brings the same discipline and dedication to every client relationship. He is deeply involved in the Colorado Springs community, serving on the boards of Penrose St. Francis Hospital, Cheyenne Mountain Zoo, and the Greater Colorado Springs Economic Development Corporation.
